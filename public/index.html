Controls
'mouse': move your cursor to adjust the angle of your shot
when your angle is bound change your shot power by moving your cursor left to right
'W': press once to bind angle and press again to fire
'S': press to unbind angle
'A': move left
'D': move right
'Z': switch to Light Shell
'X': switch to Heavy Shell
'C': switch to Explosive Shell
Rules
1. Each player takes turns to move and fire
2. You cannot move or fire during the other player's turn
3. The game is over when one player's health drops below 0
